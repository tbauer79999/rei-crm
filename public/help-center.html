📚 First Week Success Plan

Day 1: Initial Setup & Configuration

▼
  1. Complete Company Settings: Navigate to Settings → Company Information. Fill in your business details, operating hours, and industry type. This helps AI tailor conversations.
  2. Purchase Phone Numbers: Go to Settings → Phone Numbers. Purchase at least one number for your first campaign. Consider getting numbers with local area codes for better response rates.
  3. Invite Your Team: Visit Settings → Sales Team. Add team members who will receive hot leads. Set their permissions and notification preferences.
  4. Configure AI Basics: Check Settings → AI & Automation. Review default escalation thresholds (when leads become "hot"). Start with defaults, optimize later.
  5. Upload Knowledge Base: In Settings → AI Knowledge Base, upload any PDFs, documents, or FAQs about your business. This helps AI answer specific questions.

✅ Best Practice: Test Phone Number

After purchasing a phone number, text it from your personal phone to ensure it's working. You should receive an auto-response from the AI.

Day 2-3: First Campaign Creation

▼
  1. Create Test Campaign: Go to Campaign Management → Create Campaign. Name it "Test Campaign - [Your Business]"
  2. Select Campaign Goal: Choose the goal that matches your business:
    • Real Estate: "Qualify Sellers"
    • Staffing: "Recruit Candidates"
    • B2B: "Book Demos"
    • General: "Lead Nurture"
  3. Configure AI Personality: Select or create an AI personality. Start with "Professional" for B2B or "Friendly" for consumer-focused campaigns.
  4. Assign Resources: Select your purchased phone number and assign a sales team member who will receive hot leads.
  5. Upload Test Leads: Create a CSV with 10-20 test leads (can be fake numbers or team members). This lets you see the system in action safely.

⚠️ Important: Start Small

Always test with a small batch before uploading hundreds of real leads. This helps you fine-tune messaging and ensure everything works as expected.

Day 4-5: Monitor & Optimize

▼
  1. Check Control Room: Visit Pipeline Control Room daily. Monitor:
    • System Health indicators (all should be green)
    • Lead Journey funnel (see where leads drop off)
    • AI Optimization metrics (response rates, timing)
  2. Review Conversations: Click on individual leads in the Dashboard to read AI conversations. Look for:
    • Natural conversation flow
    • Appropriate responses to questions
    • Proper hot lead identification
  3. Adjust AI Settings: Based on observations:
    • If too many false "hot leads": Increase escalation threshold
    • If missing opportunities: Lower threshold or adjust AI instructions
    • If responses seem off: Update knowledge base or AI personality
  4. Set Up A/B Test: Create your first A/B test to optimize performance:
    • Test different opening messages
    • Compare professional vs friendly tone
    • Experiment with follow-up timing

Day 6-7: Scale Up Operations

▼
  1. Upload Real Leads: Now that you've tested and optimized, upload your first batch of real leads (50-200 recommended)
  2. Create Production Campaigns: Set up campaigns for different lead sources or goals:
    • Separate campaigns for different lead types
    • Geographic segmentation if needed
    • Different approaches for cold vs warm leads
  3. Train Your Team: Schedule a meeting to show sales team:
    • How to access hot leads
    • How to read conversation history
    • Understanding lead scores and sentiment
    • Proper handoff procedures
  4. Set Up Reporting: Configure weekly reports in Analytics to track:
    • Conversion rates by campaign
    • Cost per hot lead
    • Sales team performance
    • ROI by lead source

Understanding Lead Statuses

Every lead in the system has a status that automatically updates based on their interaction with your AI:

Status Indicator Description Next Action
🔥 Hot Lead Red/Fire Lead shows high interest, urgency, or buying signals. AI has identified them as sales-ready. Immediate sales follow-up required
💬 Engaging Orange Lead is actively conversing with AI, asking questions, showing interest. Let AI continue nurturing
↩️ Responding Green Lead has responded to initial outreach, conversation is active. Monitor for escalation
❄️ Cold Lead Blue No response yet or minimal engagement. May need different approach. Wait for follow-up sequence
🚫 Unsubscribed Gray Lead has opted out of communications. No further contact allowed. Remove from active campaigns

Factors That Influence Lead Score

  • ✅

    Response Time & Frequency

    Leads who respond quickly and maintain conversation flow score higher. Multiple exchanges indicate genuine interest.

  • ✅

    Message Length & Quality

    Detailed responses with questions score higher than one-word answers. AI analyzes engagement depth.

  • ✅

    Sentiment Analysis

    Positive language, enthusiasm, and agreement signals increase score. Negative sentiment or objections lower it.

  • ✅

    Buying Signals

    Keywords like "how much", "when can we start", "interested in learning more" significantly boost scores.

  • ✅

    Urgency Indicators

    Time-sensitive language ("need this soon", "by next month") increases hot lead probability.

    📊 Campaign Performance Optimization

    Key Performance Indicators

  • ✅

    Initial Response Rate

    Percentage of leads who reply to first message. Industry average: 15-25%. Below 10%? Check your opening message and timing.

  • ✅

    Conversation Depth

    Average messages exchanged per lead. More messages = higher engagement. Aim for 4-6 messages before hot lead status.

  • ✅

    Hot Lead Conversion

    Percentage of engaged leads that become hot. Should be 10-20% of responders. Too high? You might be missing opportunities. Too low? Tighten criteria.

  • ✅

    Speed to Hot

    Time from first contact to hot lead status. Faster is usually better - indicates strong initial messaging and proper lead targeting.

  • ✅

    Sales Acceptance Rate

    Percentage of hot leads your sales team confirms as qualified. Below 70%? Adjust AI escalation criteria.

    • ✅ Optimization Checklist

    • Run A/B tests on opening messages monthly
    • Review AI conversations weekly for quality
    • Get sales team feedback on lead quality
    • Adjust escalation thresholds based on outcomes
    • Test different AI personalities with same list
    • Monitor unsubscribe rates (should be under 2%)

    💬 Conversation Intelligence

    What AI Analyzes in Every Message

  • ✅

    Sentiment Score (-1 to +1)

    Measures emotional tone of the message. Positive sentiment (>0.5) indicates interest and engagement. Negative sentiment (<-0.3) might indicate frustration or disinterest. AI adjusts approach based on sentiment trends.

  • ✅

    Urgency Detection

    Identifies time-sensitive language like "need this soon", "by next week", "ASAP". High urgency combined with positive sentiment fast-tracks hot lead status.

  • ✅

    Hesitation Indicators

    Detects uncertainty phrases like "not sure", "maybe", "I'll think about it". AI responds with reassurance and addresses concerns without being pushy.

  • ✅

    Qualification Signals

    Looks for budget mentions, timeline discussions, decision-maker identification. These signals increase lead score dramatically.

  • ✅

    Engagement Depth

    Measures message length, question asking, and topic exploration. Deeper engagement indicates genuine interest vs. polite responses.

    • AI Response Strategies

    Lead Behavior AI Strategy Example Response
    Short, minimal responses Ask open-ended questions "I'd love to understand more about what you're looking for. What's most important to you in [solution]?"
    Asking specific questions Provide detailed answers + soft CTA "Great question! [Detailed answer]. Would you like to discuss how this could work for your specific situation?"
    Expressing concerns Address directly + social proof "I understand your concern about [issue]. Many of our clients felt the same way initially. Here's how we address that..."
    High interest signals Move toward commitment "It sounds like this could be a great fit! The best next step would be a quick call with our specialist. What's your availability like this week?"
    Going cold/delayed responses Re-engagement attempt "Hey [Name], just wanted to check back in. I know things get busy. Is this still something you're interested in exploring?"

    🧪 A/B Testing Mastery

    The A/B testing framework lets you optimize every aspect of your AI conversations with scientific precision.

    What You Can Test

  • ✅

    Opening Messages

    Test different hooks, value propositions, and approaches. Example: Professional intro vs. casual greeting vs. question-based opener.

  • ✅

    AI Tone Variations

    Compare conversion rates between Professional, Friendly, Casual, or Custom personalities on the same lead list.

  • ✅

    Follow-up Timing

    Test immediate vs. 1-hour vs. next-day follow-ups. Find the sweet spot for your audience.

  • ✅

    Message Sequences

    Compare different conversation flows. Should you ask for contact info early or build rapport first?

  • ✅

    Value Propositions

    Test different benefits and pain points. Price-focused vs. service-focused vs. results-focused messaging.

    • A/B Testing Best Practices

    ✅ Testing Guidelines

    • One Variable at a Time: Only test one element to clearly identify what drives results
    • Sufficient Sample Size: Need at least 100-200 leads per variant for statistical significance
    • Run Full Cycle: Let tests run 7-14 days to account for different response patterns
    • Document Everything: Keep notes on what you tested and why for future reference
    • Apply Learnings: Winner becomes new control for next test

    Understanding Test Results

    Confidence Level What It Means Action to Take
    95%+ ✅ Statistically significant winner Implement winning variant across all campaigns
    70-94% 🟡 Trending toward significance Continue test for more data
    Below 70% ⏳ Insufficient data Need more participants or time

    🔧 Troubleshooting Guide

    Quick solutions to common issues. Most problems can be resolved in minutes with these troubleshooting steps.

    🚨 Critical Issues - Immediate Action Required

    AI Not Sending Any Messages

    ▼
    🚨
    Impact: No lead engagement, potential revenue loss

    Diagnostic Steps:

    1. Check Campaign Status: Is it Active? Is AI turned ON?
    2. Verify Phone Number: Is one assigned? Is it active/verified?
    3. Review Lead Status: Are leads assigned to the campaign?
    4. Check Time Settings: Are you within business hours?
    5. Message Throttle: Have you hit daily/hourly limits?
    6. System Health: Check Control Room for red indicators

    Quick Fixes:

    • Toggle campaign off/on to reset
    • Verify phone number by texting it yourself
    • Check Settings → Messaging for throttle limits
    • Ensure leads have valid phone numbers with country code

    Hot Leads Not Being Detected

    ▼

    Common Causes:

    • Escalation threshold set too high (>85)
    • AI personality mismatch with audience
    • Campaign goal doesn't match lead intent
    • Conversations ending too early

    Solutions:

    1. Review recent conversations - are leads showing interest?
    2. Lower threshold to 70 temporarily and monitor
    3. Check if AI is asking qualifying questions
    4. Ensure follow-up messages are enabled
    5. Run A/B test with different approach

    High Unsubscribe Rate (>3%)

    ▼

    ⚠️ Risk: High unsubscribes can damage sender reputation

    Address immediately to prevent delivery issues

    Diagnostic Questions:

    • Is your opening message too aggressive?
    • Are you texting outside business hours?
    • Is lead source quality poor?
    • Frequency too high? (>1 message/day)
    • Message content spammy or misleading?

    Immediate Actions:

    1. Pause campaign temporarily
    2. Review and soften opening message
    3. Verify lead consent/source quality
    4. Reduce follow-up frequency
    5. A/B test friendlier approach

    ⚠️ Common Issues & Solutions

    CSV Import Failing

    ▼

    Error: "Invalid file format"

    • Save as CSV (not Excel format)
    • Use UTF-8 encoding
    • Remove any formulas or formatting
    • Check for hidden columns/rows

    Error: "Headers don't match"

    • Download fresh template from platform
    • Headers are case-sensitive
    • Remove any trailing spaces
    • Don't add/remove columns

    Error: "Invalid phone numbers"

    • Include country code (+1 for US)
    • Remove special characters: ()-.
    • Format: +15551234567
    • No extensions or letters

    Sales Team Not Getting Notifications

    ▼

    Check These Settings:

    1. Team Member Status: Active and Available?
    2. Campaign Assignment: Assigned to specific team member?
    3. Notification Settings: Email/SMS enabled in their profile?
    4. Email Delivery: Check spam folder
    5. Phone Number: Correct number with country code?

    Create a test lead and manually mark as hot to trigger notifications

    📞 Phone Number Management

    Phone Number Strategy

  • ✅

    Local vs Toll-Free

    Local numbers get 3x better response rates. Get numbers in your target market's area codes. Toll-free good for national brands only.

  • ✅

    Number Rotation

    For high-volume campaigns, rotate between multiple numbers to maintain deliverability. 500-1000 messages/day per number maximum.

  • ✅

    Dedicated vs Shared

    Each campaign needs its own number. Never share numbers between different campaign types - confuses leads and AI.

  • ✅

    Number Warming

    New numbers should start slow. Begin with 50-100 messages/day, increase by 50 daily until target volume.

    • ⚠️ Compliance Requirements

    • Only text numbers with explicit or implied consent
    • Honor opt-outs immediately (automatic)
    • Include business identification in first message
    • Respect quiet hours (no texts 9pm-8am local time)
    • Maintain records of consent for 4 years
